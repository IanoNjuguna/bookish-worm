# Doba — Design System & Branding

## 1. Brand Identity
**Doba** is the future of collaborative music routing. It empowers creators to mint, stream, and split revenues instantly across top L2 networks (Base, Arbitrum, Avalanche) without middlemen.

### Core Values:
- **Autonomy**: Pure creation without intermediaries.
- **Collaboration**: Instant, transparent revenue splits.
- **Innovation**: Multi-chain, cutting-edge DeFi music tech.
- **Fluidity**: Seamless routing of value and sound.

---

## 2. Visual Language

### Aesthetic: "The Void & The Pulse"
The design language is centered around a dark, immersive "void" contrasted with vibrant, energetic "pulses" of color. It feels futuristic, premium, and alive.

### Color Palette:
- **Primary (Void)**: `#050505` (Deep space black/dark grey)
- **Accent (Cyber Pink)**: `#FF007F` (High-energy, neon pink for call-to-actions)
- **Secondary (Ether Blue)**: `#00D2FF` (Cool cyan for highlights and transitions)
- **Background Gradient**: Void-to-Cyber-Pink gradients for depth.

### Typography:
- **Headings**: `Outfit` or `Inter` (Bold, clean, futuristic sans-serif)
- **Body**: `Inter` (Highly readable, modern)
- **Mono**: `JetBrains Mono` (For technical/developer-centric data displays)

---

## 3. Motion & Interaction (GSAP)

The interface is highly dynamic, using GSAP for a premium feel:
- **Parallax Background**: Subtle movement of background assets (like `hero-bg.jpg`) to create depth.
- **Floating Particles**: Interactive or parallax-driven particles representing "data pulses" or "music notes."
- **Scroll Triggers**: Elements fade in, scale, or slide into position as the user explores the page.
- **Staggered Reveals**: Hero content and feature cards reveal themselves with a timed, rhythmic sequence.

---

## 4. Components Architecture

### Navbar
- Glassmorphic backdrop (`backdrop-blur`).
- Minimalist navigation with high-contrast hover states.

### Hero Section
- Bold "GSAP-hero-title" with high impact.
- Animated "GSAP-hero-card" representing a music NFT or routing flow.
- Interactive CTA buttons with hover-glow effects.

### Features Section
- Grid-based layout with "GSAP-feature-card" components.
- Subtle hover transforms and glow intensities.

### How It Works
- Alternating horizontal slide animations for a narrative flow.
- Visual steps representing the "Mint -> Stream -> Split" journey.

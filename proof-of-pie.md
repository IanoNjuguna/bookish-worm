## Doba World

Doba World enables musicians to mint and fractionalize music NFTs into music tokens. The fractional music tokens are distributed to buyers through parameterized distribution pools that enforce creator payments, treasury fees, and anti-drainage protections.

## What the Product Does

Show the user-facing problem and the product in plain language.

- **Who the user is**  
 Doba World is a Web3 music platform built on the Cardano blockchain that connects artists, producers, and musical collaborators directly with listeners. It solves the long-standing industry problems of opaque revenue reporting, delayed royalty distribution, and middlemen high-jacking creator profits.

- **What the user can do**  
 `Musicians & Creators`: Independent artists, songwriters, audio engineers, and beatmakers looking to distribute their music fairly.

 `Music Fans`: Listeners and collectors who want to support their favorite artists directly and own unique digital music assets.

- **What value the user gets**  
 `Creators` can mint their songs as Music NFTs and program automated smart contracts that define exact revenue splits among collaborators.
  
 `Fans` can stream tracks, discover new talent, and collect music tokens that give them access to the music records they like forever.

- **Where payment happens**  
  Payment happens directly on-chain via the Cardano blockchain using smart contracts. Transactions are executed instantly via compatible Cardano wallets.

## How a User Buys the Product

Doba bypasses traditional web checkouts (like entering a credit card number) and relies entirely on decentralized interactions.

Here is exactly how a fan/buyer completes a purchase:

Step 1: Set Up and Fund a Cardano Wallet

Step 2: Connect to the Doba Platform

Step 3: Select the Music NFT then click the collect" button next to the music asset.

Step 4: Authorize the purchase.

Step 5: Instant Delivery and Splitting
The Cardano blockchain processes the transaction within seconds:

To the `Buyer`: The Music NFT is instantly delivered into their crypto wallet, granting them verified ownership and permanent access of the digital track.

To the `Creators`: The smart contract instantly divides the ADA payment, sending the respective revenue percentages directly to the wallets of the artists and producers involved in the song.
